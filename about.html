We are more than an organization.

Yewa Youth Elites is a movement — built on culture, driven by purpose, and committed to raising a generation of Yewa youth who are ready to lead.

Who We Are

Yewa Youth Elites (YYE) is a youth-focused, non-profit organization dedicated to promoting the cultural heritage of the Yewa people while driving sustainable youth development across Yewa communities.

🌱

Founded to empower young people with the mindset, skills, and opportunities they need — without losing their cultural identity.

Our Story

Culturally grounded. Globally ready.

We believe knowing where you come from gives you the strength to go anywhere. YYE adopts a community-driven and impact-focused approach by working closely with schools, local leaders, and partners to design programs that address real challenges faced by young people.

Education & Mentorship — School outreach, academic guidance, and mentorship that opens doors for Yewa students.

Cultural Promotion — Events and initiatives that celebrate and preserve the rich Yewa identity and heritage.

Youth Empowerment — Skills development, career guidance, and personal growth for the next generation.

Community Development — Programs that foster unity and sustainable growth across Yewa communities.

Our Purpose

Driven by mission. Guided by vision.

Our Mission

Empowering Yewa youths through education, mentorship, and culture.

We build capacity, preserve cultural values, and create pathways for young Yewa people to thrive — both within their communities and in the wider world.

Our Vision

A generation of Yewa youths who are grounded, equipped, and globally competitive.

We see a future where every Yewa youth has the identity, knowledge, and opportunity to lead a meaningful, impactful life.

Core Objectives

Promote and preserve Yewa culture and heritage

Support youth development through education and mentorship

Encourage innovation, entrepreneurship, and career growth

Provide academic support and guidance for students

Bridge the gap between young people and opportunities

Inspire youths to think beyond limitations

How We Work

Our approach to impact.

Through mentorship, exposure, and access to opportunities, YYE unlocks the full potential of Yewa youths.

01

Community-driven design

Every program is shaped by listening to the real challenges young people and communities face — not assumptions.

02

School & leader partnerships

We work directly with schools and local leaders to deliver programs where they matter most — on the ground.

03

Mentorship & exposure

We connect Yewa youth with mentors and opportunities that expand their sense of what's possible for their future.

04

Impact-focused execution

We are deliberate about outcomes — every initiative is designed to create meaningful, measurable change in young lives.
